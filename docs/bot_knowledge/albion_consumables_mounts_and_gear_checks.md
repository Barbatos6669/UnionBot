# Albion Consumables, Mounts, And Gear Checks

Keywords: albion food potion mount cape gear check overcharge repair durability bag weight inspect ready set swap consumable poison resist gigantify invis omelette stew sandwich pork pie

Many failed groups are not missing skill; they are missing basic preparation.

Before content:

- Repair gear.
- Bring the required food and potion.
- Bring a cape that matches the role.
- Bring the correct mount for the content.
- Check bag weight before leaving.
- Bring swaps if the LFG or caller asks for them.
- If overcharge is expected, make sure the caller said so and you understand the
  cost/risk.

Common consumable logic:

- Damage food supports kill pressure.
- Defensive food supports survival.
- Cooldown food supports frequent utility or rotations.
- Resistance or gigantify-style potions help survive burst.
- Poison-style potions help single-target pressure.
- Invisibility-style tools are often used for escape, positioning, or setup.

Mount logic:

- Cheap fast mounts are common for normal roams.
- Tankier or carrying mounts are used for transport.
- Expensive mounts increase risk and should match the value of the job.
- If the caller asks for a specific mount, follow the LFG.

Bot caution:

Do not claim exact item values or current potion/food meta. Tell members to
follow the LFG comp and in-game item text.
