# Home Guild Guide Library Index

Keywords: guide library guide category new player starter videos learning path index tutorial beginner help read first

Use this when members ask where to learn Albion basics or which guide channel
to read first.

Guide category structure:

- Guide index: start here when a member does not know what to read.
- New player start: first week path, tutorial, Destiny Board, fame, silver, and
  finding useful content.
- Builds and gear: how weapons, armor, IP, spec, food, potions, mounts, and
  build swaps work.
- PvP basics: safe fight learning, cooldowns, defensive usage, escapes,
  target selection, and death reviews.
- Silver economy: buy orders, sell orders, refining, crafting, black market,
  transport risk, and avoiding bad assumptions about instant prices.
- Gathering and transport: gathering gear, tools, mounts, banking, escape
  habits, and route risk.
- Mists and Roads: solo/duo Mists, Knightfall Abbey, Roads portals, Roads
  chests, route scouting, and risk management.
- Blackzone roaming: lethal open-world loops, solo roaming, group roaming, and
  farming points.
- Faction warfare: Martlock faction content, outposts, faction groups, faction
  economy, and faction builds.
- Guild objectives: season points, Might/Favor, Power Cores, Energy Vortices,
  castles, territories, hideouts, and CTA-style objectives.
- Comms and callouts: common voice calls, target calls, regroup/reset language,
  and what members should do when a caller speaks.
- Shotcalling: how to lead content, make calls, use terrain, assign backup
  callers, and review fights.
- Survival and death review: zone safety, anti-gank habits, banking discipline,
  and how to learn from death logs.
- Useful tools: official guides/wiki, market tools, build tools, killboards,
  and calculators.
- Avalonian dungeons: Home Guild T6 Avalonian dungeon comp and tutorial.

UnionBot routing:

- If the question is "where do I start?", point to the guide index and new
  player start.
- If the member asks "what should I wear?", use builds and gear.
- If they ask "how do I make silver?", use silver economy or gathering and
  transport depending on context.
- If they ask "how do I stop dying?", use PvP basics, blackzone roaming, or
  survival and death review, blackzone roaming, or gathering and transport.
- If they ask "how do I help the guild score?", use blackzone roaming and guild
  objectives.
- If they ask "how do I lead content?", use shotcalling, comms and callouts,
  and PvP basics.
- If they ask "what site do I use?", use useful tools.

Answer style:

- Give one next step. Do not overwhelm new players with every system at once.
- Mention that videos can go stale after patches; the guide channels are a
  starting point, not a lawbook.
- Prefer Home Guild policy and current LFG/caller notes over old videos.
