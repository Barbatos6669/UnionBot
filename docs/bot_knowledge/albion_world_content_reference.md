# Albion World Content Reference

Keywords: albion content world open world dungeon dungeons solo group static randomized corrupted hellgate mists knightfall abbey brecilien roads avalon avalonian depths abyssal depths arena crystal arena expedition hce world boss pve pvp lethal nonlethal

Use this file when members ask what content exists, what group size fits, where
to go, or how risky a content type is.

Open world:

- Open-world content is unscripted and risk depends on zone type, location,
  time, scout information, and enemy traffic.
- Blue/yellow open-world content is safer learning and economy space.
- Red/black open-world content can become full-loot PvP. Treat unknown players
  as hostile unless faction/party/guild/alliance rules say otherwise.

Randomized and group dungeons:

- Solo randomized dungeons are aimed at solo or very small groups.
- Group dungeons and static dungeons are generally group PvE spaces, often
  around 3-5 players, but real requirements depend on tier, zone, build quality,
  and enemy PvP risk.
- Static dungeons are known locations. Because players know where they are,
  PvP/gank risk is higher than a hidden random dungeon.

Corrupted Dungeons:

- Corrupted Dungeons are solo dungeons with PvE and potential PvP invasion.
- Difficulty and lethal status matter. Do not assume all corrupted content has
  the same loss rules.
- Infamy, IP caps, and build rules can matter. Verify current difficulty rules
  in game.

Hellgates:

- Hellgates are accessed through rituals and create instanced PvP/PvE content.
- Common sizes include 2v2, 5v5, and larger formats depending on current rules.
- Some Hellgates are lethal and some are non-lethal. Check before bringing
  expensive gear.
- IP caps/softcaps affect effective power.

Mists, Knightfall Abbey, and Brecilien:

- Mists are solo/duo-focused open-world instances.
- Mists entered from safer regions can be non-lethal; lethal Mists are real risk.
- Knightfall Abbey appears inside the Mists and is temporary dungeon-style
  content rather than an endless farm space.
- Brecilien is the city connected to Mist progression and access.

Roads of Avalon:

- Roads are a network of portal-linked zones with travel, gathering, chests,
  small-group objectives, and hideout/rest logistics.
- Roads routes are volatile. Portal connections, charges, and time remaining
  matter.
- For UnionBot, route questions belong to the SSO/routes workflow only when the
  user asks about routes, portals, SSO, scouting, or Roads travel.

Avalonian Dungeons:

- Avalonian dungeons are endgame group PvE content, usually requiring real
  organization, assigned roles, gear checks, and a caller.
- Treat them as planned guild content, not casual open LFG unless leadership is
  ready to organize composition and loot.

Abyssal Depths:

- Abyssal Depths are small-group dungeon content with PvE and PvP pressure.
- They are intended as high-risk, high-reward small-group encounters, with rules
  that differ from normal open-world PvP. Verify current death/loss rules in
  game before telling someone what they will lose.

Arena and Crystal Arena:

- Arena and Crystal Arena are instanced 5v5 PvP matchmaker modes.
- They are not normal full-loot open-world fights.
- They are useful for role practice, mechanics, target calling, and teamfight
  discipline, but they do not fully teach open-world risk.

Expeditions and HCE:

- Expeditions are city-accessed PvE instances.
- Hardcore Expeditions use maps/levels and require more strict builds, swaps,
  coordination, and experience.
- HCE advice should be conservative. Do not invent exact current meta builds.

World bosses and open-world PvE:

- World boss areas and valuable PvE zones attract PvP risk.
- If the reward is obvious, assume other groups may scout it too.
- Bring scouts, escape plans, bank often, and do not farm beyond the group's
  ability to defend.
