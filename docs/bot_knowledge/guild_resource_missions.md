# Guild Resource Missions

Keywords: resource resources gathering gatherer gatherers mission stockpile raw materials ore hide fiber wood stone stack stacks bounty line-item shopping Resource Lines

Resource missions are small guild objectives for stocking materials and paying
members who need cash.

How they work:

- Officers or logistics staff can post a resource mission as a bounty.
- If the bounty has a Resource Lines or Items & Materials button, members
  should use that button to claim one line instead of claiming the whole bounty.
- Each line may represent a resource type, stack count, and payout per stack.
- Members gather or buy the materials, deposit them in the requested guild
  storage location, then submit proof.
- An officer confirms delivery. The bot can credit the member's silver balance,
  and staff settle the payment in-game.

Important:

- A posted payout is usually a cap or target, not an unlimited buy request.
- If the mission says max 3 stacks per resource, do not bring more unless an
  officer asks for it.
- Partial turn-ins may need officer handling if the button line expects a full
  stack count.
- Resource missions are good for newer or lower-economy members because they
  give them a concrete objective that helps the guild stockpile.
