# Albion Mists, Hellgates, Corrupted, And Arena

Keywords: albion mists mist duo mists corrupted dungeon cd hellgate hellgates hg crystal arena arena solo duo 2v2 5v5 instanced pvp practice ip softcap lethal nonlethal rat

These modes are useful for PvP practice, but each has different expectations.

Mists:

- Solo or duo open-world style content.
- Often opportunistic. Pick fights carefully and do not assume every shiny
  objective is worth dying for.
- Escape, matchup knowledge, and greed control matter.

Corrupted dungeons:

- Solo dungeon/invasion style content.
- Good for learning duels, cooldown trading, and matchup discipline.
- Builds can be very matchup-specific, so do not treat one build as universal.

Hellgates:

- Small-team PvP/PvE content with coordinated comp needs.
- Follow the group size and role needs exactly.
- Communication and cooldown timing matter more than random damage.

Crystal Arena:

- Instanced team fight practice.
- Good for learning healing, peeling, target focus, and positioning.
- It is useful practice, but open world adds mounts, chokes, third parties, and
  loot pressure.

Bot caution:

Lethal/nonlethal status, IP rules, and mode details can change. Tell members to
check the queue/event UI and current LFG.
