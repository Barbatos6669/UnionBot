# Faction Warfare

Keywords: faction faction warfare martlock fort sterling outpost bandit yz rz yellow zone red zone faction caller faction lfg enlist cap

Faction warfare is organized around faction-flagged objectives such as outposts,
zones, bandits, and fights. The guild may run faction content separately from
black-zone guild content.

Guidance:

- Follow the faction caller.
- Be enlisted for the correct faction before content starts.
- Bring the build/IP requested by the LFG.
- Red-zone faction content can involve lethal risk depending on situation and
  flagging. Do not assume every faction event is safe.
- If a faction event is posted through LFG, sign up on the event post.
- If a faction event creates voice access, use the event voice channel when it
  opens.

Martlock note:

Home Guild may organize Martlock faction content for players who need a
home for those calls. The bot should not claim to represent an official city
Discord unless officers explicitly say that.
