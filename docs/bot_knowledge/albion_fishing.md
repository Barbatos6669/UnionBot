# Albion Fishing

Keywords: albion fishing fish fisherman rod bait seaweed salad freshwater saltwater rare fish biome zone tier t7 level 7 deadwater eel deadriver eel catch gathering journal fame

Use this file when members ask where or how to catch a fish, how fishing works,
what bait does, or why a higher-tier fish is not showing up.

Quick correction:

- If a member asks for "Deadriver Eel", they probably mean **Deadwater Eel**.
- Deadwater Eel is a **T7 rare freshwater fish**.
- Practical answer: fish T7-T8 freshwater in forest, swamp, or highland biomes.
  Bring a T7-capable fishing setup if possible: fishing rod, bait, fisherman
  gear, seaweed salad, and a safe escape setup.
- Bait increases bite speed, but it does not force one exact rare fish. Rare
  fish are still RNG, so expect to move between fishing spots and keep trying.

Fishing basics:

- Fishing is a gathering profession. It gives fishing fame, fills fisherman
  journals, and creates fish used for food, sauces, and market profit.
- Fish are tied to water type, zone tier, and sometimes biome.
- Freshwater and saltwater have different fish tables.
- Higher-tier fish usually require higher-tier zones and higher fishing
  progression. For a new member, tell them to match their rod/unlock to the
  fish tier they are hunting.
- Fishing gear and seaweed salad improve yield and efficiency, but in lethal
  zones survival matters more than greed.

How to answer "how do I catch X fish?":

1. Correct obvious name mistakes without being rude.
2. Identify fish tier and whether it is freshwater or saltwater.
3. Tell them the zone tier and biome/water type they need.
4. Recommend rod/unlock, bait, fishing gear, seaweed salad, and safe travel.
5. Warn them that rare fish are RNG and may take time.

Example answer:

"You probably mean Deadwater Eel. That is a T7 rare freshwater fish. Fish T7-T8
freshwater in forest, swamp, or highland zones. Use a T7-capable fishing setup,
bring bait and seaweed salad if you have it, and keep moving between fish spots.
Bait speeds bites, but the eel itself is still RNG."

Lethal-zone fishing advice:

- Do not bring a fishing set you cannot afford to lose into red, black, or Roads
  content.
- Bring an escape weapon, proper boots, invisibility/defensive options, and a
  mount that fits the route.
- Bank after a good catch. Fish are not profit until they reach safety.
- If fishing near black-zone objectives or roads, assume scouts/gankers may be
  watching common exits.
