# Weapon Roles And Content Pings

Keywords: weapon roles weapon tree weapon trees content roles content pings ping roles pvp roads faction healer tank dps support comp build shotcaller self assign

UnionBot has two related self-assign role systems:

- Content roles decide what content pings a member wants.
- Weapon-tree roles show what weapon lines a member can realistically bring to
  organized content.

## Content Roles

Content roles are for pings.

Examples:

- PvP
- Faction Warfare
- Ganking
- Small Scale
- ZvZ
- Hellgates
- Crystal Arena
- Duo Mists
- Abyssal Depths
- Roads
- Group Dungeons
- Static Dungeons
- Avalonian Dungeons
- World Boss
- Tracking
- Gathering
- Transport
- Economy

Members should pick content they actually want alerts for. Picking everything
makes pings noisy and makes turnout harder to read.

## Weapon-Tree Roles

Weapon-tree roles are for comp planning, not pings by themselves.

UnionBot uses broad trees instead of one role per weapon because per-weapon
roles would become noisy and stale after balance changes.

Current tree groups:

- Frontline and melee: Sword, Axe, Mace, Hammer, Spear, Dagger, Quarterstaff,
  War Gloves.
- Ranged damage: Bow, Crossbow, Fire Staff, Frost Staff, Cursed Staff.
- Healing and support: Holy Staff, Nature Staff, Arcane Staff, Shapeshifter
  Staff.

Members should pick trees they are willing to show up on, not every tree they
own at low spec.

## How To Answer Members

If a member asks where to pick content pings:

- Send them to the content roles panel.
- Tell them content roles control pings and event alerts.

If a member asks where to pick weapon roles:

- Send them to the weapon-tree role panel, usually the same channel as content
  roles unless staff moved it.
- Explain that weapon roles help shotcallers build comps and find swaps.

If a caller asks why weapon roles matter:

- They make it easier to see who can fill tank, healer, DPS, support, and swap
  roles before content starts.
- They reduce last-minute “who can play X?” spam.
- They help build cleaner LFGs and more realistic comps.

## What Not To Say

- Do not say a weapon-tree role proves mastery. It only means the member says
  they can bring that tree.
- Do not promise a player will be selected for a comp because they picked a
  weapon role.
- Do not ping weapon roles unless a staff/caller workflow explicitly supports
  that ping.
