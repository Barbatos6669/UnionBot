# Albion Content Types

Keywords: content type faction ganking roads avalonian static dungeon group dungeon world boss hellgate crystal arena mists duo mist corrupted gathering transport economy zvz small scale

Common content types in the server:

- Ganking: catching and killing targets in open world. Needs mobility, catch,
  purge, damage, and discipline.
- Small scale: coordinated open-world fights with a small group. Needs roles,
  comp, healer, caller, and clean engages.
- ZvZ: larger fights where positioning, clumps, defensives, and shotcalling are
  more important than individual chasing.
- Roads / Avalonian Roads: portal-chain content. Groups should track route,
  zone names, TTL when known, and extraction plan.
- Static dungeon: open-world dungeon farming. Can become PvP quickly because
  other groups can invade.
- Group dungeon: PvE dungeon for groups. Still bring PvP awareness if entering
  lethal zones.
- World boss: high-value PvE area that can attract fights.
- Hellgates: instanced PvP/PvE content with set group sizes and comp needs.
- Crystal Arena: instanced PvP practice. Useful for learning roles and buttons,
  but not the same as open-world pressure.
- Mists / Duo Mists: solo or duo open-world style content with opportunistic
  fights.
- Gathering: resource collection. Gatherers need escape gear and should avoid
  moving like free loot.
- Transport: moving items between markets or hideouts. Needs route planning,
  scout awareness, and risk management.
- Economy / Market: buy orders, sell orders, crafting, hauling, and arbitrage.

When members ask what content they can join:

- Tell them to check active LFG posts and content role pings.
- Tell them to sign up on the event post if they plan to attend.
- If they are unsure about a build, tell them to ask the event caller in the
  event thread or LFG post.
