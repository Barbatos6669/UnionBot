# Albion Death Review Playbook

Keywords: albion death review died die killed death black zone red zone ganked gank dismounted caught bubble mount timer defensives purge reflect healer position overextend split group vod mistake regear

Use this file when a member asks why they died, what they should have done, or
whether a death was avoidable.

Fast answer shape:

- First: confirm the risk. "If it was lethal content, losing gear is normal."
- Second: find the failure point. Was it route, mount, position, cooldowns,
  greed, comp, call discipline, or information?
- Third: give one concrete fix for next time.

Common death causes:

- Route greed: hauling/farming through dangerous zones without a scout or escape
  plan.
- Overloading: being too heavy to move away after dismount.
- Bubble mistake: breaking zoning/entry safety before deciding where to go.
- Dismount mistake: getting hit or forced off mount in a bad place.
- Choke mistake: walking into narrow terrain where catch/bomb is easy.
- Cooldown waste: using boots, cleric, cleanse, resist, or defensives before the
  real danger.
- Reflect mistake: continuing to damage into reflect.
- Overextension: chasing beyond healer/support range.
- Split group: leaving the caller or being late to mount/zone/reset.
- No bank discipline: carrying too much loot for too long.

Question checklist:

- What zone/content was it?
- Were you mounted, dismounted, overloaded, or on cooldown?
- Did you still have boots/defensive/potion?
- Were you with the group and in healer range?
- Did the caller say hold, reset, mount, or zone?
- Did the enemy force your defensives before the real burst?
- Was the route scouted?

Good short answer:

"That sounds like a route/position death. In BZ, assume every choke is watched.
Next time bank earlier, stay mounted, keep bubble until you know your path, and
do not cross dangerous chokes overweight without a scout."

Regear caution:

- Do not promise regear. Tell them to check the regear policy/channel and event
  rules.
- If it was an approved guild event, they can submit according to policy.
- If it was solo greed or wrong gear, it may not qualify.
