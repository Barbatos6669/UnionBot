# Home Guild Discord Channel Directory

Keywords: discord server channels categories where post route routing channel map server layout start here union board union hall content chat content ops martlock faction alliance guest voice archive

This document explains where members should go inside the Home Guild
Discord. Use the live server map and configured channel mentions for exact
links, but use this document for purpose and routing logic.

Core rule:

- If a member asks where something belongs, route them to the most specific
  channel for that workflow.
- Do not send normal LFG questions to SSO routes, market, bounties, flex, or
  officer spaces unless the user is actually asking about that workflow.
- Do not mention private leadership channels to non-officers.

Start Here:

- Server guide: overview of how the Discord is organized.
- Rules: server rules and conduct expectations.
- Register here: Albion identity verification and screenshot flow.
- Apply to guild: only for people applying to join HomeGuild in-game.
- Staff applications: for members applying to help as staff or caller roles.
- Help ticket: public help/escalation path when a member is stuck.

Union Board:

- Announcements: official guild announcements.
- Event board: where members create organized content and prime timer claims.
- Looking for group: where event posts appear and where members sign up or
  withdraw.
- Content roles: where members pick PvP, Faction Warfare, Ganking, Roads,
  Gathering, Economy, and other content pings.
- Votes: polls, surveys, and leadership feedback.

Guides:

- Avalonian Dungeons: T6 Ava tutorial, comp guidance, IP bands, pull flow, boss
  notes, scouting checklist, and learning-run expectations.

Union Hall:

- English chat, Spanish chat, Portuguese chat: general member chat.
- Flex: loot, kills, economy wins, and member flex posts.
- Hall of fame: highlighted achievements and hourly achievement summaries.
- Union lore: roleplay/lore posts.
- Bot commands: safe place to test bot commands and AI/moderation behavior.

Content Chat:

- Ava Roads: Roads of Avalon discussion.
- Mists: solo/duo mists discussion.
- Hellgates: hellgate discussion.
- Ganking: ganking discussion.
- Fame farm: dungeon/static/world boss fame farming.
- Gathering: gathering and resource talk.
- Crafting: crafting, refining, and economy production talk.
- Crystal Arena: Crystal Arena discussion.

Content Ops:

- Content planning: planning content before it becomes an LFG.
- Shotcalling SOP: shotcaller process, expectations, and call structure.
- Comps and builds: builds, comps, IP requirements, and gear planning.
- Regear request: regear submissions and reimbursement workflow.
- Battle VODs: recordings, reviews, and after-action learning.

Resources:

- Albion patch notes and news: game updates.
- Union market: arbitrage, buy-order/sell-order ideas, and economy posts.
- Bounty board: guild bounties, resource missions, kill bounties, and payout
  tracking.
- Member suggestions: ideas for the server, bot, guild systems, and features.
- SSO routes: Roads/portal route scouting only. Do not route LFG creation here.

ALLY Alliance:

- Alliance info: alliance requirements, expectations, and recruitment info.
- Alliance announcements: official alliance announcements.
- Alliance events: alliance-wide event/LFG posts.
- Alliance chat: normal alliance coordination.
- Guild leaders: alliance guild-leader coordination; do not route normal members
  here unless they are a guild leader or officer.

Martlock Faction:

- Martlock info: faction warfare info and setup.
- Martlock LFG: faction warfare events and LFG posts.
- Faction chat: normal faction talk.
- Martlock comps: faction warfare builds and comps.

Guests:

- Guest info: what guests can access and how guest participation works.
- Guest chat: guest-facing conversation.
- Welcome/goodbye: automated lifecycle messages. Do not route normal questions
  here.

Voice:

- Travelers Lounge: general voice.
- Content Voice join-to-create: content/event rooms.
- Alliance join-to-create: alliance rooms.
- Faction join-to-create: faction warfare rooms.
- Guest join-to-create: guest rooms.
- Vibe join-to-create: social/off-topic rooms.
- Event voice channels may be temporary and may require signup before joining.

Guild Feed:

- Activity feed: condensed activity and points summaries.
- Kill bot: Albion kill feed.
- Death bot: Albion death feed.

Common routing examples:

- "How do I register?" -> register here.
- "I uploaded a screenshot wrong" -> register here, click Register again, then
  upload when the bot asks.
- "How do I make an LFG?" -> event board.
- "Where do I sign up?" -> looking for group event post.
- "Where does faction warfare go?" -> Martlock LFG or faction chat.
- "Where does alliance content go?" -> alliance events.
- "Where do I pick pings?" -> content roles.
- "Where do builds go?" -> comps and builds.
- "Where is the Ava dungeon guide?" -> Avalonian Dungeons guide.
- "Where do VODs go?" -> battle VODs.
- "Where do I ask for regear?" -> regear request.
- "Where do market routes go?" -> union market.
- "Where do SSO route updates go?" -> SSO routes.
