# Albion PvE, Dungeons, And World Boss Content

Keywords: albion pve dungeon dungeons static group dungeon solo dungeon avalonian dungeon ava dungeon hce expedition world boss fame farm famefarm ff pull tank healer dps skip loot chest invade

PvE content is for fame, loot, practice, and sometimes baiting fights. In lethal
areas, PvE can turn into PvP quickly.

Common PvE content:

- Solo dungeons: lower coordination, mostly solo learning/fame.
- Group dungeons: group PvE with tank/healer/DPS structure.
- Static dungeons: open-world fame farms with high traffic and PvP risk.
- Avalonian dungeons: larger coordinated PvE with stricter role needs.
- HCE / expeditions: structured PvE where comp and execution matter.
- World boss zones: high-value PvE areas that can attract PvP groups.

Avalonian Dungeon planning:

- For T6 Ava full clears, use the dedicated T6 Avalonian Dungeon guidance and
  the "T6 Ava Full Clear" comp in the comp library.
- Treat T6 as a learning/consistency tier: clean pulls, clean comms, boss
  discipline, and safe extraction matter more than greed damage.
- Default T6 Ava core: 1H Hammer, Incubus, Ironroot main healer with Celestial
  Censer, Holy healer with 1H Holy + Celestial Censer, Ironroot party healer,
  Great Arcane, 1H Arcane silence, Ironroot BMS, Blazing, Lightcaller,
  Mistpiercer, then ranged DPS flex.
- Comfort band: about 1220-1280 average IP, with 1300+ preferred on tank,
  main healer, Holy healer, key support, and DPS weapon/core pieces.

PvE role basics:

- Tank controls pulls and positioning.
- Healer keeps the group alive and avoids pulling extra danger.
- DPS follows target focus and avoids standing in bad mechanics.
- Supports bring utility if the comp needs it.

Lethal PvE warnings:

- Bring PvP awareness even to PvE events.
- Watch entrances, scouts, and suspicious movement.
- Do not overfill bags and greed every chest if extraction is unsafe.
- If invaded or contested, follow the caller.
