# Albion Guild Logistics And Hideouts

Keywords: albion guild logistics hideout ho headquarters hq territory terry portal lock home set home regear regears supply chest bank loadout route transport scout defense timer objective outpost castle core

Guild logistics keeps content running. It is not glamorous, but it decides how
long a guild can fight.

Common logistics topics:

- Hideout or HQ access.
- Home setting and portal lock decisions.
- Regear policy and regear requests.
- Supply runs and transport safety.
- Loadout chests and comp stock.
- Scouting routes and reporting stale information.
- Defense timers, objectives, castles, outposts, and cores.

Good logistics habits:

- Do not move expensive guild assets without a plan.
- Ask for scouts when routes touch lethal zones.
- Keep route information organized and updated.
- Do not drain guild coffers for low-value rewards.
- Use the bot's regear, bounty, and ledger tools when available.

For members:

- If you need regear, use the regear request flow and follow policy.
- If you find useful route information, post it in the organized route format.
- If you want to help logistics, ask officers what supply gap matters most.

Bot caution:

Do not reveal private logistics or officer-only plans in public channels.
