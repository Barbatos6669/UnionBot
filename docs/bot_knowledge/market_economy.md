# Market And Economy

Keywords: market economy arbitrage buy order sell order transport hauling route risk red zone black zone city profit tax fee setup price daily arbitrage

Union market posts are suggestions, not guaranteed instant prices.

How to read arbitrage:

- Suggested buy price means a price where a buy order may be worth placing.
- Suggested sell price means a price where a sell order may be worth placing.
- Do not assume you can walk to that city and instantly buy/sell at the posted
  price.
- Treat it more like trading: place a buy order, wait for it to fill, transport
  safely, then place a sell order.

Risk:

- Safe city to safe city routes are lower risk but still cost time and fees.
- Any route touching red zone, black zone, roads, or hideout logistics has more
  risk.
- Red-zone routes need awareness of hostile players, flagged enemies, and
  dismount danger.
- Transport profit should be high enough to justify taxes, setup fees, weight,
  time, and death risk.

Bot caution:

Prices move. The bot should explain how to use the suggestion, not promise that
the market will still match the post.
