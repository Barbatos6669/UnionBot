# Albion Roles And Comp Builder

Keywords: albion roles comp composition build builder tank healer support dps rdps mdps bruiser clap bomb pierce purge cleanse defensive caller shotcaller small scale zvz gank roads static dungeon faction avalonian

Use this file when members ask what role to play, how to build a comp, why a
group needs certain roles, or whether a lineup makes sense.

Role-first thinking:

- Tank/catch: starts fights, controls enemies, peels allies, and creates clumps.
- Defensive tank/support: protects backline, saves cleanse/defensives, controls
  enemy divers.
- Healer: keeps the group alive and positions so the group can actually receive
  healing.
- Pierce/debuff: makes called targets take more damage.
- Purge: removes enemy buffs/defensives so the kill window works.
- Ranged DPS: follows damage calls while staying in healer range.
- Melee DPS/bruiser: pressures and finishes but must not dive alone.
- Bomb/clap DPS: saves major damage for the called clump/window.
- Scout: prevents the group from walking blind into danger.
- Looter/banker: keeps value from being lost after wins.

Small group shell:

- 1 catch/tank.
- 1 healer or sustain support.
- 1 purge or pierce if possible.
- 2-4 damage dealers that can hit the same window.
- Optional scout for Roads/BZ/static/faction movement.

Gank group shell:

- Catch/dismount tools.
- Purge or anti-escape.
- Burst to finish before help arrives.
- Mobility to chase and disengage.
- Scout information and clean comms.

PvE/static shell:

- Tank that can hold mobs and survive pulls.
- Healer with enough energy/sustain.
- DPS with safe positioning and interrupt awareness.
- Escape plan if in lethal zones.
- Bank plan if fame farm turns profitable.

Faction/ZvZ shell:

- Caller.
- Engage tanks.
- Defensive tanks/supports.
- Healers.
- Ranged DPS/bomb DPS.
- Cleanses/purges/pierces as assigned.
- Scouts and regroup discipline.

How UnionBot should answer comp questions:

- Ask content type, group size, lethal/non-lethal, and IP floor.
- Do not invent exact current meta builds.
- Give role shell first, then tell them to use the current comp post/caller for
  exact weapons.
- If they have no healer, warn that the content must be low-risk or very small.
- If they have no catch, warn that enemies may simply leave.
- If they have no purge/pierce, warn that kills may stall into defensives.

Good short answer:

"For a 5-man BZ roam, think 1 catch, 1 healer, 1 purge/pierce, and 2 damage.
The goal is not random DPS; it is catch -> purge/pierce -> burst -> reset."
