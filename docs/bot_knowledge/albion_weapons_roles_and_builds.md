# Albion Weapons, Roles, And Builds

Keywords: albion weapon weapons build builds role roles tank healer support dps ranged melee bruiser clap brawl kite catch purge pierce cleanse defensive swap helmet armor shoes cape potion food

Albion builds should be understood by role first, not by weapon name alone.

Common roles:

- Tank / catch: starts fights, catches enemies, peels for allies, and creates
  clumps. Often uses plate armor and control weapons.
- Healer: keeps the group alive, positions safely, and saves cleanse or major
  heals for real pressure.
- Support: brings purge, cleanse, defensives, utility, or engage tools depending
  on the comp.
- Ranged DPS: deals damage from safer positions but must not outrange heals.
- Melee DPS / bruiser: pressures targets and follows engages, but can die fast
  if it dives without support.
- Bomb / clap DPS: saves big cooldowns for the called damage window.

Build checklist:

- Weapon matches the role requested by the LFG.
- Helmet, armor, shoes, cape, food, potion, and mount are included.
- Defensive choices match the content. Escape sets, ZvZ sets, gank sets, and PvE
  sets are not interchangeable.
- If a comp lists swaps, bring them or ask the caller before joining.

How to answer build questions:

- Ask what content they are joining.
- Ask whether they need tank, healer, support, ranged DPS, melee DPS, or economy
  gear.
- Point them to the event comp or caller for exact item choices.

Bot caution:

Do not invent current best-in-slot. Weapon balance changes often. Give role
logic and point to guild comps for exact builds.
