# Albion Ganking And Anti-Ganking

Keywords: albion gank ganking anti gank catch dismount scout purge invisibility boots mount road choke red zone black zone transport gatherer escape bait split target nametag

Ganking is about controlling movement and forcing a target to make mistakes.
Anti-ganking is about denying that setup.

Ganking basics:

- Find targets with scouts, map awareness, and good route prediction.
- Catch before dumping damage.
- Chain control carefully; random crowd control can waste the kill window.
- Purge or force boots/defensives before committing all damage.
- Do not split too far chasing one target unless the caller approves it.

Good gank group pieces:

- Catch / dismount pressure.
- Purge or forced defensive pressure.
- Damage that can finish before the target escapes.
- Mobility to keep up.
- A caller who decides when to chase and when to stop.

Anti-ganking basics:

- Avoid predictable chokes and exits when possible.
- Watch for scouts, nametags, corpses, and players waiting in odd places.
- Keep mount health high.
- Do not waste boots or defensives too early.
- If transporting, use route planning and scouts for valuable loads.

Bot caution:

Do not encourage harassment outside the game. Keep advice focused on Albion
gameplay.
