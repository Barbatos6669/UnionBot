# Bounties And Rewards

Keywords: bounty bounties payout paid reward silver confirm paid enemy target scout route shopping task treasury coffers coffer

Bounties are guild-funded tasks. They can include scouting, shopping, enemy
targets, or other officer-approved objectives.

General rules:

- Do not promise payout amounts unless they are visible on the bounty post.
- If a bounty says it requires approval, an officer must approve or confirm it.
- If a bounty says payment must be confirmed, use the confirm-paid flow/button
  when available.
- Guild coffers matter. Rewards should encourage useful activity without
  draining treasury on low-value tasks.

For kill or enemy bounties:

- Ask for proof such as killboard link, screenshot, or other evidence required
  by the post.
- Do not invent an enemy list. If no target is listed, tell the member there is
  no active public target known to the bot.

For scouting / SSO route bounties:

- Routes should be posted through the route board format when possible.
- Include portal chain, destination, scout, notes, and TTL/close time when known.
- If a route is closed or wrong, update the route board instead of making a new
  messy post.
