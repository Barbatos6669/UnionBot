# Albion Roads And Avalonian Content

Keywords: albion roads avalonian ava portal route chain scout scouting ttl hideout ho chest green blue gold core aspect gather roam extraction collapse deep road rest zone

Avalonian Roads are portal-chain zones with shifting routes and mixed PvE/PvP
opportunities.

Roads basics:

- Portals change, so route info can go stale.
- Always track the route chain, not just the destination.
- Include TTL or close time when known.
- Have an extraction plan before going deep.
- Roads can contain chests, gathering, aspects, cores, hideouts, and roaming
  fights.

Avalonian Dungeon note:

- T6 Avalonian Dungeons are larger coordinated PvE raids, not casual roads
  chest groups.
- Use the dedicated T6 Avalonian Dungeon guidance when asked about full clears,
  boss mechanics, IP bands, or dungeon comps.
- The normal home-guild T6 Ava default is Hammer + Incubus + Ironroot main healer +
  1H Holy healer + Ironroot party healer + Great Arcane + 1H Arcane + Ironroot
  BMS + Blazing + Lightcaller + Mistpiercer, with Badon/Longbow/Skystrider,
  Light Crossbow/Weeping/Siege, and Great Frost as flex options.

Good roads planning:

- Starting location.
- Portal chain / zone names.
- Destination or objective.
- Scout name.
- Time remaining if known.
- Danger notes: hostile guilds, blobs, camps, or recent deaths.

Group advice:

- Stay together unless the caller splits scouts intentionally.
- Do not chase through unknown portals without permission.
- Bank loot when reasonable.
- If a road closes, update the route board and do not send members into stale
  info.

Bot caution:

Routes are time-sensitive. Do not claim a route is still open unless current
route data says so.
